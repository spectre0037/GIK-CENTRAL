About Us

We are a student-led initiative committed to capturing the spirit, legacy, and everyday magic of GIKI. Whether you're a prospective student, a curious parent, an alum, or just someone fascinated by one of Pakistan's top engineering institutions — this is your place to explore it all.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate GIKIans who believe that GIKI is more than just academics — it’s a legacy, a lifestyle, and a lifelong journey. From late-night hostel debates to society events that shake the campus, from intense study sessions in the library to peaceful walks under the starlit sky — we live it all, and we love to share it.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve, promote, and amplify the true spirit of GIKI — one story, one memory, one experience at a time.
We aim to:
Showcase the vibrant academic and co-curricular life at GIKI.
Connect current students, alumni, and future GIKIans.
Celebrate the achievements, events, and everyday moments that make GIKI extraordinary.
At GIKI Central, we’re driven by purpose: to be the digital heartbeat of GIKI — honest, inclusive, and ever-evolving.
