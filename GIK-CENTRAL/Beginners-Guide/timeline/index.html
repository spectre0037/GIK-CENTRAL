FRESHERS GUIDE THROUGH GIKI

Education System at GIKI:
A Comprehensive Overview

Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology (GIKI) is one of Pakistan's most prestigious engineering institutes, renowned for its academic rigor, state-of-the-art facilities, and student-centered approach. Here's a detailed look at the education system and campus life at GIKI.

Academic Structure and Credit Hours
Brief overview of credit hours

GIKI follows a semester system, with two main academic semesters: Fall and Spring. Each program is structured around a credit hour system, where:

1 credit hour of theory = 1 hour of lecture per week.

1 credit hour of lab = 3 hours of lab work per week.

Students are typically required to complete 130-150 credit hours over four years to graduate, depending on the degree program. These include core courses, electives, labs, and final year projects. Continuous assessment is carried out through quizzes, midterms, assignments, labs, and final exams and an attendence of 80% is required to pass each course.

Student Life
Hostels, Mess, and Medical Center

Hostels

GIKI offers on-campus accommodation in multiple hostels:

For Boys : Hostel 1-6 & 8-12

There are only double 2-bed rooms in hostel 8-12
meanwhile hostel 1-6 contains single room and a few 2-bed room

First Year students stay in hostel 11 and 12

Second Year students stay in hostel 9 and 10

Third and fourth Year students stay in hostel 1 to 6

For the first two years, students are bound to live in double bed room hostels
From third year onwards ,Single rooms are alloted for students lying above certain CGPA
Every fourth year student is given a single room

For Girls : Hostel 7 ,13 and seperate houses ( C-types )

Each hostel is equipped with internet access, and common rooms.
After every two days ,laundry is taken from the hostels

LAUNDRY CHARGES : 80rs per piece (washed & pressed)

MESS

FOR BOYS : One central mess with different halls for all boys

FOR GIRLS : Mess Inside hostel

MESS CHARGES : Approximately : 500 - 600 rs per day(all meals included)

You can take mess food every day and if you wish to skip mess then you will have to book yourself out for that particular day

Society Culture in GIKI
Details of all the society culture in GIKI

start date - end date

There are approximately 30 - 40 societies in GIKI covering various domains (sciences/arts/adventure)
Students can join them according to their intrest

Process of joining a society

In order to join a society you will have to first give an interview which will be a volunteer interview that the society will anounce VIA social media/Door to door campaign in hostels/email
First the society will announce the selected volunteers who will become a temporary part of society
After volunteer period is over , the societies will announce there induction call interviews in which they will permanently select the students for the society
Every society consists of an Executive council from 3rd/4th year who run the society with other members
